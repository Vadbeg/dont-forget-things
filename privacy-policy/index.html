Privacy Policy

Effective as of January 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Leaving Home Checklist ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Checklist Data

Leaving Home Checklist stores your checklist items and settings. This includes:

  • Checklist item names and categories
  • Your custom lists (e.g., "Leaving Home", "Travelling")
  • Completion history and timestamps

Important: All checklist data is stored entirely on your device. Your data is never uploaded to our servers or any third-party services.

Photo Access

The app offers an optional photo confirmation feature that allows you to attach photos to checklist items for visual confirmation (e.g., confirming you locked the door). When you use this feature:

  • Photos are captured and stored locally on your device
  • Photos are only used within the app for your personal reference
  • Photos are never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties

Usage Data

We may collect non-personal information to improve our app:

  • App crashes and errors
  • General usage patterns and feature engagement

Data Storage

All your personal data remains on your device at all times. The app stores the following locally:

  • Your checklists and items
  • Completion history
  • Photo confirmations (if used)
  • Your preferences and settings
  • Subscription status

We do not have access to your checklists, photos, or any personal data stored on your device.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple - For App Store and in-app purchases via StoreKit

Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your information. All data processing happens locally on your device, ensuring your personal information never leaves your control.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.
